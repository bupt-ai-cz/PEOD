# Term of Use and License

## Note

PEOD presents pixel-aligned Event–RGB pairs acquired using a coaxial imaging setup with a Prophesee EVK4 camera and a Hikvision MV-CS050-10UC camera at 1280×720 resolution and 30 Hz. The dataset contains manual annotations for six categories with more than 340k bounding boxes, includes over 57% challenging illumination data, and covers diverse driving scenarios including urban roads, suburban roads, complex intersections, tunnels, and highways.
